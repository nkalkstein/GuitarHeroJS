HOW TO PLAY

First of all, this game is inspired by GuitarHero. Balls will appear randomly on the 4 vertical blue, green, red and yellow lines, going from top to bottom. Each string is associated with a keyboard key: S for the blue string, D for the red string, F for the red string and G for the yellow string..
The goal is to press the right button at the good time (when the ball is aligned on the black line). Everytime you get some points and the difficulty will increase. If you let a ball pass then you loose ! So be reactive.

And now it's up to you to do the best score !
