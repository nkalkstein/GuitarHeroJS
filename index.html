HOW TO PLAY

First of all, this game is inspired by GuitarHero. Balls will appear randomly on the 4 vertical ropes blue, green, red and yellow and go from top to bottom. Each string is associated with a keyboard key: S for the blue string, D for the red string, F for the red string and G for the yellow string.
The goal is the press the right button at the good time (when the ball is on the black line). Everytime you get some points, the difficulty will be increased But if you let a ball pass then you loose ! So be reactive

And now it's your turn to do the best score ;)
